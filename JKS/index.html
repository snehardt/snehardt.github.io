About My Website

This site is a place where you can come and have fun with some jokes. When you come to this site, please plan to spend a while. I developed this site with the sole intention of making people happy. Here, you will not only find irresistibly-funny jokes, but you will also find jokes that are so poorly written that you can't help but laugh. Read them, memorize them, and share them with your friends. No doubt, you will put a smile on anyone's face whose willing to listen. Jokes brighten the dimmest of days and the darkest of nights.

Get ready to laugh! It is easy to pick up and use whenever you want. This is a family-friendly site where you and others can spend hours sharing and laughing at these jokes. Use these jokes for family gatherings, talent shows, or other fun-filled activities. Now get exploring and enjoy a laugh on me.
