Dad Jokes

Dad Jokes are great to tell everyone. They may not get the best reaction every time, but they can sure change somebody's mood. These jokes are great for any situation to lighten up the mood and to bring joy for those around. They can be so cheesy that you can't help but smirk or make a weird face. I hope you enjoy some of these jokes and make someone's day!
