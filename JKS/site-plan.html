Overview

Purpose

I am making a website. It will be about jokes and puns. I want my website to put smiles on faces and hope that they have a better day than they were having. It will be very basic with a few puns and categories of jokes. Some people might look and this and share it to others, and I can have this spread throughout the world. Would a joke make you happy? If we have enough jokes, there is always one that will make you laugh. This will make the world better and happier.

Audience

My audience is everyone parents of kids or single adults. They will come to this sight to help someone who has had a bad day. They want to calm someone down, or to brighten someone's day. They may have had a hard time at work or just want to crack a joke. It does not matter what job they have, but it does matter what has happened during the day. I'm looking for poeple that can have a good time telling jokes. This site is the best place for them.

Content

Home page

The best thing about this site is that people will come here to have a good time and try to find jokes to tell with their friends and family. Anyone who is down, or knows someone who is having a bad day, this site will help them cheer up and make their day better. The best way to brighten someone’s day is to make them smile, and the best way to make someone smile, is to tell jokes and to have a good time. This world need to become a better place for everyone. The only thing you need is to have a good mind and be ready to laugh. It is easy to pick up and use whenever you want to. This site is family-friendly sop anyone can come here and visit with no worries about their kids. It is also a great place to come and hang out as a family, or for a talent show to share groups to practice comedians. My site will be the best in the jokes industry because you can use any of them whenever you want. It will be easy to look at and pick up any of the jokes. This site will be here for anyone who wishes to impress people with jokes, or just to brighten someone’s day.
